The Problem We Solve

Performance anxiety affects millions.

Performance anxiety affects millions of people — athletes, students, and professionals alike.

Research shows that guided imagery and mental rehearsal can reduce anxiety, but current treatments still depend mainly on self-reporting and therapist intuition.

Market Analysis

What's Broken in Today's Market

01.

Existing Solutions Are Not Focused on Anxiety

Most existing solutions target relaxation, not performance anxiety specifically.

02.

No Objective Measurement During Exposure

Therapists rely on self-reporting during exposure sessions — no real data.

03.

Limited Accessibility

Most tools require clinics or professional supervision to function.

Our Solution

Our Solution, Reimagined

A real-time platform for anxiety exposure and progress tracking — built on physiological data, not guesswork.

📊

Data-Driven Visual Exposure

Real-time exposure adapted based on live physiological signals — not self-reports.

🎯

Personalized Training Control

Custom scenarios, session settings, and progress tracking tailored to each user's anxiety level.

📚

Knowledge & Support Hub

Practical guidance, expert articles, and ongoing support tools — all in one accessible place.

Key Features

Built to Help Users Manage Anxiety

Real-time monitoring, personalized exposure, progress tracking, and accessible support tools.

❤️

Real-Time Monitoring

Track physiological signals during exposure sessions using the MAX30100 sensor and Raspberry Pi.

🧠

Personalized Exposure

Adjust scenarios to each user's needs and anxiety level for effective, gradual exposure.

📈

Personal Progress Insights

Monitor changes over time — see recovery times shorten and improvement become measurable.

Knowledge & Support Center
